Digital Designs for Cub

These samples works are a collection of work for local grocery store, Cub. Included in the samples are responsive emails, social posts, and carousel ads. Previously created by an outside agency, emails were not responsive and were made from bits of the printed ad. I brought these in-house and created a responsive template which can be easily updated on a weekly basic. As 60% of our guests are viewing these emails on their mobile device, we want their experience to be good. Relevant content from our banners are also posted on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram to engage customers and give them a little something extra.

Highlights:

  • Social posts have images that are fresh and engaging our target audience.
  • Content should add value to customers who subscribe to our chanels and enhance their experience with Cub.
  • Email content should be flexible and easy to view and interact with regardless of what device you're using.
  • The whole campaign, whether for a week or a season, should be seamless and belong together.
