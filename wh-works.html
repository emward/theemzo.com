Wild Harvest Works

Wild Harvest is one of Supervalu's Private Brands, which gives us the freedom to dig in a have fun with images and content! Wild Harvest prides itself on being a family-focused brand, "free from" all sorts of things like antibiotics, artificial flavors and colors, trans fats and more. That means all design should be fresh, colorful, fun and focused on healthy options.

Highlights:

  • Social content provides customers who are health-conscious with ideas and tips.
  • Colorful, playful nature of the brand should also appeal to kids. Eating better food should be fun!
  • Provide Facebook subscribers with unique products and recipe ideas free from funky ingredients.
