My name is Emily.
Pleased to meet you.

I'm a creative, resourceful Senior Digital Designer who loves clean lines, challenging projects and the pursuit of the perfect solution. I have a wide variety of experience including web design, UX design, front-end development, art direction, email design and development, eCommerce, social media and other digital design. With a Bachelor of Arts degree in Graphic Design and a Post-Baccalaureate Certificate in Interactive Design & Marketing from MCAD, I bring 12 years of work experience and education to my designs.

I'm a lover of cats, coffee, the outdoors and Italian food. I never say no to a good beer flight or brewery, which I like to post up on Instagram. I live and work in the Twin Cities area with my husband and two cats. We love to travel and enjoy exploring new places and cultures. I'm also an avid reader, which I celebrate with a membership to a two-person bookclub. I can be found most nights at a yoga class, or teaching Zumba. I've been a certified instructor for about 7 years.
