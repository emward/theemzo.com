Shop 'n Save Creative

A Supervalu banner store. The creative for this brand is budget focused and price driven. The challenge is balancing enticing, colorful creative with promotional messaging to keep it engaging and clean. Emails for this banner were being created by another agency when I joined the team, and I brought these in-house to make them responsive and mobile-friendly. Developed for Salesforce, the emails are easy to access on all devices and tested across multiple email providers. The promotional messaging and other content is carried across to Facebook and other social platforms to engage customers where they want to be.

Highlights:

  • Fun, seasonal content delivered via email and social.
  • Content is designed for the banner's target audience, engaging with them to offer more than sale pricing.
  • Creating flexible templates in Photoshop makes updating content weekly a breeze.
