Cilento Photography

Cilento Photography is a professional photography studio with several locations across the country. As a designer for the brand, I created a variety of marketing materials. These materials included emails, magazine ads, large scale mall signage, Facebook images, direct mail pieces, brochures, flyers, and many other items. Creative distinction between lines of the business was essential, with Wedding, Senior and family photography all having a slightly different look and feel.

Highlights:

  • Cilento offers a Milestone package, featuring newborn photography through 2 years of age.
  • Cilento provides custom wedding photography that is unique to each couple.
  • Their Senior photography showcase each individual's own style and personality.
