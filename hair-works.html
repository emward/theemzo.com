The Hair District Design

This is one of my favorite freelance projects I've ever worked on. I love the final product - and I got to work with a super talented stylist before she became a superstar. This was a mailer for clients at the salon that could be sent seasonally to entice customers to book during slower periods of time. The final work captures my client's fresh, natural approach to style - and I got a fun portfolio piece!

Highlights:

  • Can be used as a set, however each design works as a stand alone piece.
  • Each image captures that great feeling you get when you leave the salon.
  • The copy keeps each one from being typical with a little twist.
