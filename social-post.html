Facebook Posts

These social posts can be any type of content, such as new products, recipes, or store promotions. Each one should be on brand and engage customers by having a clear message and appealing, relevant images. Making sure that each post is geared toward it's target market is the fun part.

My Process:

  • Choose images that are fresh and resonate with our target audience.
  • Clean up image color, add any product or essential elements to the post.
  • Make any changes to the image content in Photoshop to put the brand's best foot forward.
  • Add the image to the Facebook post.
