Bark Place

Bark Place was a user experience design project that I worked on during my last semester at MCAD. During the course of auditing content, writing personas, and interviewing users in the form of anyone who would let me test wireframes on them - I fell in love with the process. Bark Place might be a hypothetical business, but every step of the way was exciting informative. If you'd like to read up on the business, customer profile, competitive analysis, interviews, user testing and all the other good stuff, feel free to check it all out here.

Project Contents:

  • Initial research into the business, who their target customers would be, success metrics, branding information, and desirable features for their website.
  • Personas to help bring to life the people who would most likely use a business like Bark Place.
  • Wireframes. Lots of wireframes that were tested and tested again. Also a summary of what those tests revealed about the website functionality.
